Privacy Policy - Snazzy Paws Grooming

Effective Date: 6 September 2025

At Snazzy Paws Grooming, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and transparency. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

Purpose of Collection
We only collect personal information that is necessary to provide our grooming services, manage bookings, respond to inquiries, and improve your experience with us.

Source of Information
Where possible, we collect personal information directly from you—via our website, booking forms, phone calls, or in-person visits.

What We Tell You
When collecting your information, we’ll let you know:
- Why we’re collecting it
- How it will be used
- Who it may be shared with
- Your rights to access and correct it

Manner of Collection
We collect personal information legally, fairly, and with your knowledge and consent.

Storage and Security
We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, or unauthorized access. This includes secure digital storage and restricted access to sensitive data.

Access to Your Information
You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you. Just contact us and we’ll respond promptly.

Correction of Information
If you believe any information we hold is inaccurate or outdated, you can request a correction. We’ll make the changes or explain why we can’t.

Accuracy
We make every effort to ensure your personal information is accurate, complete, and up to date before using it.

Retention Limits
We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected. Once no longer needed, we securely delete or dispose of it.

Use of Information
We use your personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected—such as booking appointments, communicating with you, and improving our services.

Disclosure of Information
We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business, or if required by law.

Disclosure Outside New Zealand
If we transfer your personal information outside New Zealand, we ensure the receiving party has adequate privacy protections in place.

Unique Identifiers
We only assign unique identifiers (such as customer IDs) when necessary and permitted by law, and we use them responsibly.
